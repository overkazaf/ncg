通州湾科教城位于滨海园区围垦北区，未来城市功能区西部，占地约5平方公里，远期拓展到10平方公里。先期启动区约1.74平方公里，将容纳学生近2万名
Tongzhou Bay Science & Education Town is located at the north reclamation area of Binhai Coastal Park, to the west of city functional area, with an area of 5 square kilometers, and will be expanded to 10 in the future. At Phase I, about 1.74 square kilometer will be put into use to accommodate nearly 20 thousand students.
战略目标：

推动南通制造向南通创造、南通知造转型、加快经济增长方式转变，围绕南通滨海线园区“港口引领、产城融合、科教兴海”三大战略，实现短期集聚人气、中期培养人气、远期发展产业的目标，打造区域发展示范新城

Strategic objectives: Implementing three major strategies of "leading by the ports, realizing the combination of industry and city development, and developing education to enhance the industrial development", targeting on attracting the talents in the short term, cultivating talents in the middle term and achieving the sustainable industrial development in the long term; build up a new demonstrative city in Nantong area.
