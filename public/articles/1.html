南通位于中国江苏省东南部，南临长江，东濒黄海，与上海、苏州隔江相望，全市总面积8001平方公里，素有“江海门户”之称，是长三角地区“上海一小时经济圈”内最具发展活力及潜力的城市。
Located in the south-east of Jiangsu province, Nantong borders Yangtze River in the north and Yellow sea in the west, standing opposite Shanghai and Suzhou across the Yangtze River. It covers an aera of 8001 square kilometers. The city is well known for "the vital gate of Yangtze River and Yellow sea". Now it has become the most dynamic city within Shanghai one-hour economic circle in Yangtze Delta with the greatest potential.
